Facemask Detection Using OpenCV


After the COVID-19 pandemic, the need for protection mechanisms like face masks became critical. This project aims to detect face masks in live video and images using deep learning. We developed our model with the Single Shot Detector (SSD) architecture for its accuracy and speed, incorporating transfer learning. Experimental results show our model achieves 100% precision and 99% recall on test data.
